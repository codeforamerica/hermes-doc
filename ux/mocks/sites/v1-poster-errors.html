  • Texting back

    If a defendant tries to text the number back (after the initial exchange of case number and name verification), we will send a polite message instructing them to call a relevant number instead.

  • You can’t text this number. Please call 502-123-4567 for more information about your court date.

  • Name shortening

    Text messages have limits of 160 characters. In case we run over, we need to shorten the name of a person.

    You are already getting reminders about another court case, 12-M-02392 (about Lawrence Barefield McCigarett). You can’t get reminders about more than one court case.

  • We start by shortening someone’s middle name.

    You are already getting reminders about another court case, 12-M-02392 (about Lawrence B. McCigarett). You can’t get reminders about more than one court case.

  • Then, by shortening someone’s first name.

    You are already getting reminders about another court case, 12-M-02392 (about L. B. McCigarett). You can’t get reminders about more than one court case.

  • Then, by removing the middle initial.

    You are already getting reminders about another court case, 12-M-02392 (about L. McCigarett). You can’t get reminders about more than one court case.

  • And, if still necessary, by shortening the last name.

    You are already getting reminders about another court case, 12-M-02392 (about L. McCiga.). You can’t get reminders about more than one court case.
