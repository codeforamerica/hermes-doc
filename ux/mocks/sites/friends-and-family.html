• Friends and family

    We can extend our system to support friends and family to receive reminders in addition to the defendant. In this scenario, a friend types in someone else’s court case number.

    13-F-0102292

  • Our verification step becomes a clarification step.

    This is Kenisha Woods’ case. If you are Kenisha, text ME. If you are Kenisha’s friend or family member, text FRIEND. If this is a mistake, text MISTAKE.

  • The friend confirms.

    FRIEND

  • The system confirms.

    Thank you. Kenisha needs to come to court on Friday, April 8, at 1pm. We will send you a reminder text message a day before the court date.

  • 1 day before the court date

    A day before the court date, a reminder is sent. Note a different language variant.

    Kenisha’s court date is tomorrow: Friday, April 8, at 1pm, in the Hall of Justice. If Kenisha can’t make it, call 502-1234-5678 as soon as possible, today.

  • 1 day after the court date

    A day after the court date, we can send a verification text message.

    Did Kenisha Woods appear in court yesterday? If no, ask Kenisha to call 502-123-4567 immediately.
