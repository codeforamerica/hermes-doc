• Remote opt-in

    The public defender or someone else could opt in a defendant using their own phone, by texting in both the case number and the phone.

    13F0102292 502-222-5555

  • Thank you. Phone 502-222-5555 will now be receiving reminders for case 13-F-0102292 (defendant name: Misty Donaubuer).

  • Verification

    The defendant has to confirm that they want to receive the reminders.

    Someone suggested that you receive text reminders about the court case 13-F-0102292 (defendant name: Misty Donaubuer). Please text YES if you agree.

  • YES

  • Thank you. You need to come to court on Friday, April 8, at 1pm. We will send you a reminder text message a week before your court date.
