• Language variants for the intro message

    Explorations of various language variants.

    You need to come to court on Friday, April 8, at 1pm. We will send you a reminder text message a day before your court date.

  • Your court date is Friday, April 8, at 1pm. We will send you a reminder text message a day before your court date.

  • Your court date is Friday, April 8, at 1pm. We will send you a reminder text message. Failure to appear in court will result in jail time or fine.

  • Your court date is Friday, April 8, at 1pm. We will send you a reminder text message. Failure to appear in court will have severe consequences.

  • Language variants for the reminder

    Your court date is tomorrow: Friday, April 8, at 1pm, in the Hall of Justice. If you can’t make it, call 502-1234-5678 as soon as possible, today.

  • Hi, John. Your court date is tomorrow: Friday, April 8, at 1pm, in the Hall of Justice. If you can’t make it, call 502-1234-5678 as soon as possible, today.

  • With built-in interaction.

    Your court date is tomorrow, April 8, at 1pm, in the Hall of Justice. If you can’t make it, call 502-1234-5678 as soon as possible. Text YES if you understand.
