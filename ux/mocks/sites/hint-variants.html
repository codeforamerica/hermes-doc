• Hint variants for the intro message

    Explorations of various language variants. The regular one:

    You need to come to court on Friday, April 8, at 1pm. We will send you a reminder text message a day before your court date.

  • Suggesting visiting the site:

    You need to come to court on Friday, April 8, at 1pm. We will send you a re­minder text mess­age a day before. Go to the website www.add­ress.org for more info.

  • Suggesting being able to ask for HELP:

    You need to come to court on Friday, April 8, at 1pm. We will send you a reminder text message a day before. Text HELP if you want to learn more.

  • (Other accepted variants: ?, What? etc.)
