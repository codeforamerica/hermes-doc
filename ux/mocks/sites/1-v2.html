• March 8, 2013 1 month before the court date

    The defendant visits the court, receives a citation, or otherwise is presented a court date that needs to be attended.

  • March 8, 2013 1 month before the court date

    An initial text message is sent immediately.

    You need to come to court on Friday, April 8, at 1pm. We will send you a reminder text message a week before your court date.

  • March 31, 2013 7 days before the court date

    A week before the court date, a 7-day reminder is sent.

    Your court date is next week: Friday, April 8, at 1pm, in the Hall of Justice. If you can’t make it, call 502-1234-5678.

  • April 7, 2013 1 day before the court date

    A day before the court date, a final reminder is sent.

    Your court date is tomorrow: Friday, April 8, at 1pm, in the Hall of Justice. If you can’t make it, call 502-1234-5678 as soon as possible, today.

  • April 8, 2013 Court date

    The defendant shows up in court and resolves her case.
